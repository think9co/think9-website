WHO WE ARE

Designed for Indian digital native brands Think9 is a brand co-creation platform that transforms powerful ideas into authentic, profitable, and experience-led brands in key consumption categories like fashion, food and wellness, beauty, and home and decor.

We partner with passionate entrepreneurs to combine our collective learning and experience with data science, digital ethnography, and technology platforms to build brands that connect to the new generation of Indian consumers.

We are a team of experienced entrepreneurs, marquee investors, and industry professionals who have expertise and experience in building some of the most popular brands in the country.



OUR BELIEFS

Think9 is built with a culture of deep creation- rooted in grounded insight, and passionate belief; filtered through a sophisticated brands lens, detailed meticulously end-to-end, supported by technology and data stacks, and nurtured by experienced digital marketers and content creators.
